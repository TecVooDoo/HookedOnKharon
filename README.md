# Hooked on Kharon: Reeling in the Underworld

A cozy low-poly 3D fishing sim set in the Greek Underworld.

**Developer:** TecVooDoo LLC
**Engine:** Unity 6

## Concept

Play as Kharon, the eternal ferryman who just wants to fish in peace - but souls keep interrupting. Ferry the dead (grudgingly), collect obols, and upgrade gear across five legendary rivers.

**Tagline:** "Even death needs a break."
